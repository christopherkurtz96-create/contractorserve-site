Questions

Things people ask before they book.

I already use ServiceTitan / Jobber / Housecall Pro. Is this redundant?

No. Those are tools. This is a read on how your tools are actually being used, where they're letting leads slip, and what you could automate on top of them. We work inside whatever stack you already have.

Why paid? Everyone else runs free audits.

Because free audits are sales calls in a trench coat. A paid audit means we spend the week actually doing work for you, not pitching you. You get a real deliverable whether you hire us after or not.

What if you don't find anything?

If we can't identify at least $5,000/month in recoverable leaks, the audit is free. We've never had that happen, but the offer stands.

Do I have to commit to a retainer after?

No. About half of audit clients hire us to build and run the fix. The other half take the report and run it themselves. Both are fine. The audit fee gets credited to month one if you do retain us.

How much of my time does this take?

Roughly three hours over the week: a 60-min kickoff, ~30 min of pulling data access, and a 60-min walkthrough at the end. The rest is us, in our office, not yours.

Who sees my numbers?

Two people on our team. NDA on request, automatic if you're in a sensitive vertical. We don't publish financials, ever. Case studies run anonymous unless you volunteer.
