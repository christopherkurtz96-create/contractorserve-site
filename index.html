About ContractorServe

Bridging Generations in the Trades

We sit at the intersection of two urgent needs: retiring contractors who need to transition well, and hungry young operators who need a path into business ownership.

The Problem We Solve

A generation of baby boomer contractors is approaching retirement with businesses that can't survive without them. Their operations, customer relationships, and expertise all live inside their head. The result? Businesses that took decades to build get sold for the value of their trucks — or simply close their doors.

Meanwhile, there's a generation of driven young people who want to run businesses but face impossible barriers to entry. They have the work ethic, the leadership ability, and the hunger — but no pathway.

How We're Different

We're not brokers. We don't list your business and wait. We come in, roll up our sleeves, and spend six months turning your business into something that runs without you. We build the SOPs, separate the brand from the owner, clean up the financials, and structure the team.

And because we understand the trades — we've owned contractor businesses ourselves — we speak your language. No corporate consultants with clipboards. Just people who've been in your boots and know what it takes.

Our Model

We consult to systematize your business → We earn your trust through results → When you're ready to exit, we can acquire and install a trained next-generation operator who carries your legacy forward. We buy and hold for the long haul — no flipping, no gutting, no four-year exit timelines.
